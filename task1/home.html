About Us


"At Shortcakes, our journey began with a shared passion for crafting delightful Shortcakes that ignite moments of joy. Using only the freshest, hand-picked fruits and a sprinkle of love, our team of dedicated bakers pours heart and soul into every creation. From our humble beginnings to becoming a beloved local treasure, we take pride in delivering unforgettable Shortcake experiences and sharing in your celebrations. As we look to the future, we're excited to continue this journey of sweet delights. We envision expanding our offerings, exploring new flavor combinations, and reaching more dessert enthusiasts near and far. At Shortcakes, we believe that the key to a memorable dessert lies not only in its taste but also in the emotions it evokes. Each Shortcake we craft is a labor of love, handcrafted with care to ensure every bite brings a smile to your face. Beyond our delicious offerings, we take pride in fostering a warm and inviting atmosphere in our bakery. Whether you visit us in-store or order online, our friendly team is here to make your experience exceptional from start to finish."
